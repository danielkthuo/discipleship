Nurturing Your Child's Spiritual Journey From the Very Beginning

Transform your pregnancy and early parenting journey into a sacred experience. Our biblically-based curriculum helps you create a God-centered environment that shapes your child's spiritual foundation even before birth. At KCMC we believe discipleship begins way before birth. Parents are called to be the first pastors, prophets, and priests in their child's life — speaking life, shaping identity, and building a godly foundation from the womb. This two-year curriculum is designed to walk alongside families from pregnancy through the first two years, helping them disciple their children through monthly themes, scripture, spiritual activities, and intentional parenting.

VISION

To help parents spiritually nurture their child from pregnancy/birth to age 2 by establishing faith habits, developing godly character, and building deep spiritual relationships during early childhood — our critical formative period for life.

PROGRAM PURPOSE

We believe that parents, caregivers, and ministry leaders should understand that discipleship is a journey starting before birth. Through monthly spiritual prompting, practical tools, and age-appropriate faith activities, this two-year journey will prepare families to:

  1. Speak God's Word and blessing over their unborn/newborn children.
  2. Introduce spiritual rhythms and Biblical values as habits from day one.
  3. Create a Christ-centered environment in the home.
  4. Refer to parenting as an intentional effort to model godly behavior and love through consistency.
  5. Recognize that young children can still experience the love, peace, and presence of God — even before they can talk.

UTILIZING THE CURRICULUM AT KCMC

  1. Monthly Lessons (0–36 Months): Each month is centered on a biblical theme aligned with child development phases.
  2. Parent Discipleship Focus: Parents are spiritually led to grow in their primary role as faith mentors.
  3. Faith Activities: Bible verses, worship patterns, blessings, and bonding exercises to practice at home.
  4. Home & Church Partnership: The curriculum strengthens the connection between KCMC's Children Ministry and the family.
  5. Covenant Culture: Each lesson supports our Kingdom Covenant values and principles (faith, family, discipline, legacy).

At KCMC, we do not wait for children to speak before introducing them to God — we begin in the womb to raise a generation who knows the Lord from their first breath.

Key Features of the Womb to Worship Curriculum

Rooted in biblical principles that emphasize God’s presence from conception, helping parents create a Christ-centered foundation before and after birth.

Empowers parents and caregivers to become the primary spiritual mentors in their child’s early life, through intentional teaching, prayer, and example.

Holistic Growth Approach

Encourages emotional, spiritual, and relational development — supporting both parents and children to walk in God’s peace, joy, and purpose.

Frequently Asked Questions

When should I start the Womb to Worship curriculum?

You can begin at any stage of pregnancy or after your child is born. The curriculum is designed to be flexible and adapt to your family's journey.

How much time does the program require each day?

The activities are designed to fit into your daily routine, requiring just 10-15 minutes of focused time each day, with spiritual practices integrated throughout your day.

Is this program only for first-time parents?

Not at all! The curriculum is valuable for parents at any stage, whether you're expecting your first child or adding to your growing family.
